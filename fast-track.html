Fast-Track Internship Virtual Program

Our partner Youth Culture Inc. has posted a new opportunity! Their NEW FAST-TRACK Internship Summer 2021 Virtual Program is for Youth ages 15 to 20.

Gain work experience, networking opportunities, and participate in Hackathon-like teamwork—all enrolled into a one-week experience!

Youth Culture’s NEW FAST-TRACK Internship Virtual Program under our Skills & Development initiative will provide week-long internships* and career education to youth ages 15 to 20.

What do you get out of it?

☀️Certificate of Program Completion for each one-week program

☀️Experience to add to your resume

☀️Opportunity to network with your peers and industry professionals

☀️Be a part of imagining solutions to industry’s biggest problems (reminiscent of a Hackathon experience)

☀️Learn relevant professional development skills and enhance your knowledge of Accessibility and Inclusion in the Workplace

☀️Chance to win cool prizes!

This FAST-TRACK Internship program was designed for maximum engagement incorporating elements of Hackathon-type problem-solving, professional development in the workplace, networking with industry professionals, and an opportunity to connect with others while gaining relevant short-term experience to add onto resumes. Each week, a specific industry will be highlighted so students can join the weeks that are of interest to their learning journeys.
