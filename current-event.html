Innovire Summit

Welcome to Innovire's third and first-ever virtual Summit! The Innovire Summit is a conference that promotes and empowers STEM for ALL youth around the world.

Our objective is to inspire youth to innovate solutions to problems around the world using STEM by providing them with knowledge, opportunities, experiences, and resources to support them on their journey to become change-makers and leaders.

Due to COVID-19 restrictions and safety concerns, we've decided to hold this year's summit in a virtual setting. We are excited to host various speakers, interactive activities, panelists and workshops! The summit will take place from June 5th to 6th. We hope to see you there!
