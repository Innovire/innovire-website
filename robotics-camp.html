April Break Robotics Camp

During the April break of 2021, from April 12th to 17th, Innovire held a robotics camp online. We wanted to create an event where students could spend their break engaging in STEM Robotics and use their creativity to make something that would function properly in real life. We used online platforms such as TinkerCAD and VEXcode VR to do the camp.

For the Innovire Robotics Camp, an experienced Innovire member guided students to produce various programs for a robot to follow. Afterwards, we had an amazing teacher from Tomken Road Middle School host some of the days of the camp for the students. Students were able to get hands-on experience in the field of Robotics during this camp. The 5-day camp was able to inspire and excite many youth about Robotics!
