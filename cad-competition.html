Holiday CAD Competiton

Innovire's first online event was in December 2020. We realized there is an abundance of hackathons where people mainly focus on programming a solution to a problem, so we wanted to create an event where students could learn CAD (another important skill in Engineering and STEM) and use their creative thinking skills to build an interesting christmas ornament. We had access to a 3D printing service who helped us manufacture the students' designs.

The Innovire Holiday CAD Event was a huge success. We had an amazing workshop using Google Meets, where youth were able to get hands-on experience in the field of CAD and 3D printing through an online platform called TinkerCAD. Following this we held a competition to raise $300 for COVID-19 relief where youth designed 3D models of holiday decor. We had some amazing submissions and were able to inspire and excite many youth about the technology of CAD and 3D printing.
